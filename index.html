01 · About

Who I Am

A passionate Computer Science Engineering educator and researcher with over 21 years of academic experience, currently serving as a Professor & Mentor in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Coordinator Quantum Innovation Center at SR Gudlavalleru Engineering College.

My doctoral research focused on developing energy and network efficient routing mechanisms for enhancing network lifetime in Wireless Sensor-based IoT Networks — a thesis praised by both foreign and Indian reviewers at Annamalai University.

I am committed to inspiring the next generation of engineers through collaborative learning, cutting-edge research, and practical application of knowledge. My work spans IoT, cybersecurity, blockchain, quantum computing, and AI-driven systems.
